Raphael Leung

I am studying for a MSc. at the Oxford Internet Institute, University of Oxford. My graduate coursework focuses on computational social sciences and data analytics (distinction in obtained grades). My research has covered the effects of digital technologies on civil liberties (freedom of expression, privacy) and how Internet policies affect vulnerable populations. I worked as an Undergraduate Fellow at the Information Society Project at Yale Law School and as a Policy Research Fellow at the Benton Foundation. I hold a B.A. in Political Science, cum laude, from Yale University. I like running and movie quotes.
