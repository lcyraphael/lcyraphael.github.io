About Me

I am a software developer on the UK Parliament's Data and Search Team. Before that, I worked as an Associate Advisor at the Behavioural Insights Team, an Undergraduate Fellow at the Information Society Project, and a Policy Research Fellow at the Benton Foundation. I hold a B.A. from Yale University and an MSc. from the Oxford Internet Institute. I wrote my Master's thesis on the disability digital divide in the UK. I like running and movie quotes.
